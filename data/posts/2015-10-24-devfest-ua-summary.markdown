[GDG DevFest  Western Kenya  2015](https://devfest.gdg.org.ua/2015/) is now over. It was a great success.
 We are happy to share with you some facts, emotions and, of course, photos from the biggest Google Developers Conference in  Western Kenya !

On behalf of the DWK Team we warmly thank you for helping make [GDG DevFest  Western Kenya  2015](https://devfest.gdg.org.ua/2015/) a huge success.
A BIG THANK to all the volunteers from different GDG groups of  Western Kenya  for their priceless help,  thanks to all the speakers for the great speeches and sharing their experience and knowledge, and, of course, thanks to all our partners, specially, [LAKEHUB](http://www.lakehub.co.ke/).


The event took place on 28th of November at the Planet Media Cinemas Kisumu. Devfest was focused on Android, Web and Cloud technologies and also Community Hub.Over 300 people participated in the event with a mix of students and working professionals.


* 3 parallel tracks of Android, Web and Cloud technologies and also community hub.

* Participating GDG Leaders : 2 (GDG Lviv, GDG Odessa, GDG Uzhhorod, GDG Cherkasy, GDG Dnipro, GDG Kyiv, GDG Ternopil, GDG Prague, GDG Istanbul, GDG Maceio, GGD Kremenchuk).
